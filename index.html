About ME

Hi, I am Here To Help Your Next Project.

Hey there. As you saw on my first page, my name is Aleksandar Murgoski. I am 20 years old and I am from Macedonia, specifically from Struga.
My journey began after completing primary school in Struga. I had to decide where I wanted to attend high school. Without a doubt, I knew I wanted to study in Slovenia and graduate high school there. I discovered a secondary school for technical professions, but I was not sure about my choice. After reviewing all the programs the school offered, I ended up selecting the "Computer Operator" program. It was a five-year-long high school journey, and at its conclusion, I became a Computer Technician.
Over those years, I developed a strong passion for computers and everything related to IT. My desire to learn everything about the field led me to delve deeper into various areas. I began learning Adobe Photoshop, which prvided me with extensive experience in photo editing. Later, I also even got to learn Adobe PremierePro, which provided me the basics of video editing. I had the opportunity to grasp the fundamentals of web development, specifically HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
My journey has been fueled by a deep curiosity to learn and excel in the world of technology.
